Narrated Abu Huraira (رضي الله عنه); Allah’s Messenger (صلی اللہ علیہ وسلم) Said:

Allah will sa on the day of Resurrection: Where are those who love one another for m glory’s sake. Today i will shelter them in my shade, Today there is no shade but only My (Allah’s) Shade.
